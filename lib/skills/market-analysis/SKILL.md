# Market Analysis Skill

> Understand the landscape before building. Competitive analysis, feature gaps, and positioning help ensure you build the right thing.

## Analysis Framework

### Step 1: Define the Market
- What problem space are we in?
- Who are the target users/customers?
- What are the key use cases?

### Step 2: Identify Competitors
Map the competitive landscape:

| Competitor | Category | Strengths | Weaknesses | Pricing | Users |
|-----------|----------|-----------|------------|---------|-------|
| Product A | Direct | Feature X | Missing Y | Free | 10k+ |
| Product B | Indirect | Integration Z | Complex UX | $20/mo | 50k+ |

Categories:
- **Direct**: Same problem, same audience
- **Indirect**: Same problem, different audience (or vice versa)
- **Potential**: Could pivot into this space

### Step 3: Feature Gap Analysis

Create a feature matrix:
```markdown
| Feature | Us | Comp A | Comp B | Comp C |
|---------|-----|--------|--------|--------|
| Feature 1 | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Feature 2 | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Feature 3 | 🔄 | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |

✅ = has it  ❌ = doesn't have it  🔄 = in progress
```

### Step 4: Positioning
Define where we stand:
- **Category**: What type of product are we?
- **Differentiator**: What do we do that nobody else does?
- **Value prop**: Why should someone choose us?
- **Anti-positioning**: What are we explicitly NOT?

### Step 5: Opportunity Map
Rank opportunities by:

| Opportunity | Impact | Effort | Competitive Advantage | Priority |
|-------------|--------|--------|----------------------|----------|
| Feature X | High | Low | Unique | P0 |
| Feature Y | Medium | Medium | Parity | P1 |

## Output Format

```markdown
# Market Analysis: <Domain>

## Market Overview
## Competitor Map (table)
## Feature Matrix
## Our Positioning
## Top Opportunities (ranked)
## Recommendations
## Sources
```

## Rules

- **Cite sources** — don't invent competitor data
- **Be objective** — acknowledge competitor strengths honestly
- **Focus on actionable insights** — analysis that leads to decisions
- **Update regularly** — markets change, revisit quarterly
